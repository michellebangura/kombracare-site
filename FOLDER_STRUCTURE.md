# Kombra Group Folder Structure

## 11 Main Domains

### 00_GOVERNANCE/
Governance, compliance, legal, board materials, policies.

### 01_OPERATIONAL/
Day-to-day operations, processes, systems, workflows.

### 02_STRATEGIC/
Business plans, strategic plans, market analysis, competitive intelligence.
- Business_Plans/
- Strategic_Plans/

### 03_VENTURES/
All ventures organized by tier (6 tiers total).
- Tier-1_Holding_Structure/
- Tier-2_Digital_SaaS/
  - KombraCare/
  - Life_Lab/
  - [More SaaS ventures]
- Tier-3_Services_Consulting/
- Tier-4_Experiences_Physical/
- Tier-5_Products_CPG/
- Tier-6_Content_Media/

### 04_BRAND_ASSETS/
Brand materials for all Kombra sub-brands.
- Kombra_Group_Hub/
- KombraLabs/
- Kombra_Foundation/
- MichelleBangura_Personal/
- Be_Better/
- Purpose_Built_Press/

### 05_KNOWLEDGE_CONTENT/
Research, ideas, IP, learnings.
- Research/
- Ideas_Inventory/

### 06_PRODUCT_DEVELOPMENT/
Product specs, technical architecture, build documentation.
- Specs/
- Technical_Architecture/

### 07_MEDIA_PRODUCTION/
Video, audio, graphics, content assets.

### 08_GOVERNMENT_CONTRACTING/
Public sector proposals, contracts, compliance.

### 09_ARCHIVE/
Old documents, historical records, previous versions.

### 10_TEMPLATES/
Reusable templates for all document types.

## Naming Convention

`[DocumentType]_[VentureName]_[Descriptor]_v[Version].md`

Example: `PRD_KombraCare_ParentBriefing_v1.md`

All folders created and ready for documents.
